Privacy Policy for Popupify

Popupify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

The extension works entirely within your browser. It does not access or track any website content, browsing history, or user activity. It only uses local browser permissions to open and close tabs and windows.

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any remote servers. No data is transmitted to third parties.

If you have any questions, you may contact the developer.
